About Me

When I was in high school, I didn't care about the future and where I would end up in 10 years. Every day after school, I would go to these game clubs with my friends and would spend my whole daily allowance on playing video games there, and after that,I would go home , straight to my room and play even more video games there. Years passed, high school ended. Although I didn't study at all through konkour ( Iranian University Entrance exam), I got accepted by one of the top 5 universities in Iran, Ferdowsi University, at software engineering. I don't know how that happened ( people say that it's because I'm naturally good at math, but I think I was just lucky). Anyway, I started my studies there, but I still was this naive boy who only wanted to play video games , because now I was addicted to them. After two years of just passing the courses with minimum participation and almost no effort, I had a mental breakdown; I came to the realization that I am no longer that teenage boy, and these are the precious years I am wasting. Next week I dropped university.I registered for military services ( which is mandatory in Iran for every adult man), and I said to myself: "I will think about my future while I am passing these mandatory days." Long Story short, After The service was over, I participated in konkour again, but this time I was prepared. I got accepted by Tehran university at Engineering Science ( best university in Iran in this field and also overall ) and after one semester, because I had excellent grades, I got accepted by LUT to continue my studies there. Although I am just beginning and I still have a lot to learn; I see a bright future ahead of me, and I am sure I will either have my own company in 15 years or I will be CTO of a big company.

⠀ From a young age, I was always curious about how things work and how we can make the situation better. My mind always was busy with problems and the parts that could use some creativity. And honestly, I was never interested in doing ordinary tasks that everyone could do; And fortunately, I have a very logical brain and a good vision; therefore, I'm good at problem-solving. Anyway, although I don't know too much yet and luckily, there are always new things to learn; If your job involves challenges and you feel to invest in the new passionate colleague, which I can ensure will be hugely profitable, call me in.
